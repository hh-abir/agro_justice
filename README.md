
# 🌾 Agro Justice (Prototype)

> **⚠️ NOTE:** This is a **Prototype version** created specifically for competition purposes. It is not yet a feature-complete production application.

**Agro Justice** is a Flutter-based mobile solution designed to break the "middleman curse" that plagues traditional agricultural supply chains. By connecting farmers directly with end customers, we ensure farmers receive the full value of their labor while providing consumers with fresher, more affordable produce.

## 📉 The Problem
In the traditional agricultural cycle:
1.  **Farmers** sell at low prices to middlemen due to lack of market access.
2.  **Middlemen** inflate prices through multiple layers of logistics and resale.
3.  **Consumers** pay high prices for older produce.
4.  **Result:** Farmers remain in a cycle of poverty despite high production.

## 💡 The Solution
**Agro Justice** removes the layers. By providing a direct digital marketplace:
* **Direct Profit:** Farmers set their own prices and keep 100% of the earnings.
* **Price Transparency:** Customers see exactly where their food comes from.
* **Better Lifestyle:** Increased income allows farmers to invest in better tools and education.



## ✨ Key Features (Prototype)
* **Farmer Listings:** Simple interface for farmers to list their current crops and prices.
* **Customer Marketplace:** A localized feed for users to browse fresh produce nearby.
* **Direct Contact:** Integrated communication channel between buyer and seller.
* **Profile Management:** Basic identity verification for trust-building.
